What the App Does

Six modules, one platform

Every module runs on the same live data — when the clerk checks in an athlete, the staging screen updates instantly, the athlete's phone updates instantly, and the public schedule reflects it immediately.

📅
Live
Schedule of Events
The complete running order for the meet — exactly as it appears in the program. Multi-day meets are organised by day with collapsible sections. Every athlete and fan sees the same live schedule.
  • Import the meet schedule from the official meet PDF in seconds
  • Multi-day support — Day 1, Day 2, Prelims, Finals, all in one place
  • Live status per event — Upcoming, Running, Done
  • Visible to everyone — no login, no app install required
  • Admin can adjust event order or add delays on the fly
Every athlete knows exactly when their event runs — no more guessing from a paper schedule or PA announcement.
🏷
Live
Smart Check-in — Kiosk Mode
A dedicated check-in station for the venue entrance. The kiosk operator scans a bib, confirms the athlete, and the hip sticker prints automatically. Under two seconds per athlete. No paper lists, no highlighters.
  • Scan bib barcode — athlete is found instantly
  • Shows all events for that bib — operator selects the one being checked in
  • Hip sticker prints automatically with lane, heat, and bib number
  • Re-scan a checked-in bib to reprint or undo a mistake
  • Works offline on local Wi-Fi — no internet dependency
  • Multiple kiosk tablets share one printer queue — no conflicts
The hip sticker (lane, heat, bib) goes on the athlete's body — the staging team verifies it without any lookup. No wrong-lane errors at the blocks.
📱
Live
Athlete & Coach Self-Service
Any athlete or coach can look up a bib on their own phone and see every event for that athlete — heat, lane, seed time, and live status. No app to install, no account to create.
Bib 303 — Rutvi Sharma
✅ Girls 1600m · Heat 2, Lane 5 Checked In
📣 Girls 800m · Heat 1, Lane 3 At Staging
🕐 Girls 400m · Heat 2, Lane 7 Upcoming
  • Enter any bib number to see that athlete's full event list
  • See heat, lane, seed time, and live staging status per event
  • Tap "Watch My Events" to get push notifications — no login needed
  • Coach can look up any athlete on their team by bib
  • Status updates in real time as the meet progresses
Parents, coaches, and athletes all use the same screen. No separate coach app, no team login portal.
🧠
Live
Staging Assist — Clerk Dashboard
The staging clerk's command centre. Select an event, see every heat and every athlete at a glance. The screen tells you exactly who is present, who is missing, and when a heat is ready to call.
  • Select any event — see all heats with athlete list and check-in progress
  • Heat number turns green when every athlete is present — automatic alert fires
  • Tap the "First Call" card to notify all registered athletes in the event at once
  • A confirmation screen shows exactly what notification will be sent before you confirm
  • After sending, the card shows the time the call was sent — prevents double-calling
  • Each heat tile shows its current stage: At Staging, On Field, Ready to Race
First Call goes to every registered athlete for that event — all heats — with one tap and one confirmation. No individual calls, no PA needed.
🔔
Live
Athlete Communication
Athletes and coaches receive push notifications directly on their phones as the meet progresses — without any app or account. One tap to subscribe, zero effort to stay informed.

  • Athlete opens their bib view and taps "Watch My Events on This Device"
  • No app, no account, no password — one tap and they're subscribed
  • Push arrives even when the phone screen is off or the app is closed
  • Three notification types: First Call, Moving to Field, Ready to Race
  • Tap "Stop Watching" at any time to unsubscribe instantly
PA announcements become optional. Athletes know exactly when they're called — on their own phone, before the PA even speaks.
⚙️
Live
Meet Administration
The admin manages meets, imports the heat sheet, controls which meet is active across all devices, and publishes the meet to the public listing — all from a single Settings screen.
  • Create a meet with name, location, and dates — appears on the public landing page
  • Import the schedule of events from the meet PDF — AI reads the format automatically
  • Import athlete heat sheet — all events, heats, lanes, and seed times in one upload
  • Set the active meet — every device updates instantly, no manual refresh needed
  • Publish or archive meets — only published meets appear in the public listing
  • Manage user accounts and assign roles (clerk, official, kiosk, admin)
  • Delete and re-upload the schedule if the meet program changes
One admin action changes the active meet for every device in the venue simultaneously — no messaging clerks to refresh their screens.

Athlete Journey

From warm-up to finish line

Every athlete moves through five stages. Each stage is triggered by a real person — clerk, official, or the athlete themselves — and every device sees the update the moment it happens.

✅
Checked In
Kiosk Clerk or Self
Bib scanned at check-in. Hip sticker printed and handed to athlete. Each event is checked in separately — athlete can skip evening events without affecting others.
📣
At Staging
Staging Clerk
Clerk taps First Call in the dashboard. Push notification goes to every registered athlete in the event. "Report to the staging area now."
🏃
On Field
Heat Escort
Official reviews the heat, verifies bib and uniform, and taps "Move to Field." Push sent: "Athletes are moving to the field."
🏁
Ready to Race
Official confirms
Official taps "Ready to Race" when the heat is at the line. Final push sent to athletes. Self-service screen shows bright green.
🏅
Done
Official — heat level
Official marks the heat Done. Every athlete in that heat is updated automatically. Schedule advances to the next event.
📱
Any phone, any browser
Athlete status is visible to anyone who knows the bib. No app, no login. Share the link with parents before the meet.
🔔
Push even when app is closed
Notifications arrive on the lock screen. Athletes don't need to have the browser open — the alert finds them wherever they are.
🎽
Hip sticker = no wrong lanes
The sticker printed at check-in shows the athlete's lane in large print. The official at the track entry verifies it against the start list — last check before the blocks.
⚡
Per-event, not per-athlete
An athlete competing in 3 events checks in for each one separately. They can scratch an event without affecting their other check-ins. Everything stays accurate.
