Staging Clerk

You check in athletes as they arrive at the staging area and notify coaches when staging begins.

Before Race Day — Get Set Up

  • 1
    Install AppSheet from the App Store (iPhone) or Play Store (Android)
  • 2
    Sign in with your Google account — the meet director will have shared the app with your email
  • 3
    Open the app → tap Sync (↻) in the top right to download the latest heat sheet data
  • 4
    Do this before you enter the venue — once you're in the staging area you may lose Wi-Fi
Offline works fine: After syncing, the app works without internet. Check-ins are saved on your phone and upload automatically when Wi-Fi returns.

Checking In an Athlete — Scan the Bib

  • 1
    Tap the blue 📷 Scan Bib button at the bottom of the screen
  • 2
    Point your camera at the barcode on the athlete's race bib — hold steady until it beeps
  • 3
    The athlete's card appears — confirm the Event name matches the event currently staging
  • 4
    Read out the Lane (large white number) and Heat (amber number) to the athlete — direct them to the correct box
  • 5
    Tap ✓ Check In — the button turns green confirming the athlete is staged

Checking In — Bib Won't Scan?

  • 1
    Tap ⌨️ Enter Bib (the keyboard button near the scan button)
  • 2
    Type the bib number from the athlete's bib into the search bar
  • 3
    Tap the correct result — confirm the event name matches — then tap ✓ Check In
Athlete in multiple events? You may see more than one result for the same bib. Always confirm the Event name on the card before tapping Check In.

Starting Staging for an Event

  • 1
    When you are ready to begin calling athletes for an event, tap Events in the bottom menu
  • 2
    Find your event in the list — tap it
  • 3
    Tap the orange ▶ Start Staging button
  • 4
    A confirmation message appears — read the event name carefully, then tap Confirm
  • 5
    All coaches with athletes in this event receive a Telegram notification instantly

What to Do If…

Bib not found after typing
Athlete may be a late entry. Check the paper heat sheet. If confirmed, let them through and note the bib manually.
Wrong event shows up
Athlete runs multiple events. Tap the row that matches today's event before checking in.
App won't open / crashes
Force-close and reopen AppSheet. If still broken, use the paper heat sheet and tell the meet director.
Athlete already checked in
Another clerk may have scanned them. Confirm with the other clerk — athlete is already staged.

Coach

You receive a Telegram notification the moment staging opens for your event. No app required.

One-Time Setup

  • 1
    Download Telegram — free on iPhone and Android
  • 2
    The meet director will add you to the Shockwave Staging Alerts group
  • 3
    Make sure Telegram notifications are turned on on your phone — check Settings → Notifications → Telegram
  • 4
    That's it — nothing else to install or set up
No AppSheet needed. You only need Telegram. Notifications arrive automatically — you don't need to open anything or check the app.

On Race Day

  • 1
    Keep Telegram open in the background on your phone
  • 2
    When staging begins for an event your athlete is in, you will receive a push notification — it vibrates and shows on your lock screen
  • 3
    Read the event name in the message — send your athlete to the staging area immediately
  • 4
    Athletes should arrive at staging within 5 minutes of the notification

What to Do If…

Notification not received
Check Telegram → Shockwave Staging Alerts group for missed messages. Make sure phone notifications are enabled for Telegram.
Not in the group yet
Find the meet director and ask to be added to the Shockwave Staging Alerts Telegram group.
Athlete missed staging
Send them directly to the staging area — they may still be able to check in. Find a staging clerk immediately.

Timer

You monitor staging completion across all events and heats — so you know when a heat is ready to run.

Getting Access

  • 1
    Install AppSheet from the App Store or Play Store
  • 2
    Sign in with your Google account — the meet director shares the app to your email
  • 3
    Open the app and tap Sync (↻) — do this before entering the venue

Viewing Overall Staging Status

  • 1
    Tap Staging Status in the bottom menu
  • 2
    All events appear in order — each shows a check-in count (e.g. 7 / 9)
  • 3
    Green count = all athletes checked in · Amber = in progress · Red = not started
  • 4
    Tap any event to see the breakdown by heat

Searching a Specific Event

  • 1
    In Staging Status, tap the search bar at the top
  • 2
    Type part of the event name — e.g. "400" or "under Boys"
  • 3
    The list filters instantly — tap the event to see heat-by-heat status
When a heat is ready: All lanes show ✓ checked in. If any athlete is missing, coordinate with the staging clerk before starting the heat.

What to Do If…

Count seems wrong
Pull down to refresh the view. Clerks may be offline — counts update when they sync.
Athletes missing from a heat
Radio the staging clerk. The athlete may not have arrived yet. Decision to run or scratch is the starter's call.
Event not showing
Clear the search bar. Event may not have had any athletes check in yet — scroll to find it.

Announcer

You use the app to see which events are staging, who is in each heat, and when athletes are ready.

Getting Access

  • 1
    Install AppSheet from the App Store or Play Store
  • 2
    Sign in with your Google account — meet director adds your email
  • 3
    Sync the app before the meet starts

Finding Who Is in a Heat

  • 1
    Tap Staging Status in the bottom menu
  • 2
    Search or scroll to the event you are about to announce
  • 3
    Tap the event — you see each heat with athlete name, team, and lane number
  • 4
    Rows with ✓ green are physically present in staging — rows without a check are not yet arrived

Knowing When Staging Has Started

  • 1
    Tap Events in the bottom menu
  • 2
    Events with staging already started show a green ✓ and timestamp — e.g. "Staging started 10:42 AM"
  • 3
    Use this to pace your announcements — if staging just started, athletes are on their way
Quick reference during announcing: Keep Staging Status open filtered to the current event. Refresh between heats to see who is confirmed present before calling them to the line.

Athlete Quick Reference

What you need Where to find it
Athlete name & team Staging Status → tap event → tap heat
Lane assignment Shown on every athlete row (Lane column)
Who is physically present Green ✓ = checked in · dash = not yet arrived
Seed time Tap individual athlete row → Seed Time field
When staging started Events view → Started At column

What to Do If…

Athlete in heat isn't checked in
Radio the staging clerk. May still be en route. Starter decides whether to hold or scratch.
Name on screen doesn't match bib
Athlete may have swapped bibs. Go by the bib number — confirm with staging clerk.
Event missing from the list
Pull down to refresh. If still missing, check the paper heat sheet — may be a relay not tracked in the app.
